Meet the Teacher:


My name is Olga Naymushina. I am a New York State certified teacher with a Masters degree in TESOL (Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages). I received my first education in Russia (graduated with honors) and then moved to the United States where I graduated from Long Island University and have been teaching English to students whose native language is other than English in a Public School in Brooklyn, NY.


I am currently offering two courses: English - Level I and English - Level II:



    In the course of English I, you will:
  • learn vocabulary of a casual conversation (topics like family, occupation/job, interests, weather, etc.)
  • ask and answer questions.
  • read short stories, complete assignments, and have discussions about these stories
  • have multiple opportunities to practice conversational skills.


    In the course of English II, you will:
  • learn new vocabulary of the topics of your choice (health, sports, environmental problems, etc.)
  • read more complex texts, complete assignments, and have discussions;
  • have multiple opportunities to practice conversational skills;
  • develop writing skills.

* Students are expected to schedule teamwork outside of the scheduled class to improve their language skills. All the students will be receiving feedback from the teacher.
